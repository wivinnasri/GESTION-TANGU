A propos de nous

Who We Are TANGU?

TANGU means Tanganyika Guru, it is a company that works in new technologies especially in computer networks and the development of computer software. It also provides practical training for computer network enthusiasts. In the installation of networks and site interconnections, Tangu has competent technicians.



Historical view

TANGU was found by four persons who want to revolutionize how digital technologies are being practicing here in Burundi and also give a good perspective on how a Burundian society sees natural digital technologies because many Burundians most people view new technologies as a distraction for young people and those who have nothing to do.. They officially began to work in May, 24th 2021 with four members and one branch located in Bujumbura.

Location of the organization

TANGU has its headquarter office in Bujumbura town, Ntahangwa commune, in the north of Bujumbura below Emmaus house at Africana house .

Vision

TANGU has the vision to become the leading Network company in Burundi, it means well-known in Burundi territory and around the globe. The company which can depend on itself financially, technically and infrastructure etc.
